# Jotnar

Automatic journaling for people who are chronically online but have terrible memory.

Jotnar captures periodic screenshots from your devices, interprets them with a locally-hosted AI vision model, and consolidates those interpretations into natural-language journal entries. All data stays on your own hardware.
